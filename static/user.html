MediMind AI: Bridging Modern Medicine and Traditional Wisdom

"Healing is not just about medicine; it's about understanding, respecting, and bridging different ways of knowing."

MediMind AI is an innovative healthcare assistant platform that uniquely combines evidence-based modern medicine with traditional healing wisdom, creating a culturally sensitive approach to health information. This web application serves as an intelligent interface between users seeking medical information and a sophisticated AI system trained on diverse medical knowledge sources.

Modern Medicine

Our system provides evidence-based medical knowledge about symptoms, conditions, and treatments. We help you understand health concerns through contemporary healthcare

Traditional Wisdom

Drawing from centuries of healing practices, we offer insights into time-tested remedies and holistic approaches. Our knowledge includes traditional medicine from various cultures.

Cultural Bridge

We connect different health perspectives, ensuring medical information is presented in a culturally sensitive way that resonates with your background and healing beliefs

Models Used

GPT-OSS-120B : Primary model for generating culturally-sensitive, comprehensive medical explanations and bridging traditional-modern medical knowledge [model]
GPT-OSS-20B - Lightweight model for quick symptom analysis and emergency response guidance [model]

- Created mappings to modern medical terminology
